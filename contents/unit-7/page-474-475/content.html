Genre Seminal United States Document

A nation’s seminal documents are important texts written at the time that a country begins. The texts present important ideals (ideas about what is fair and just) of the government and society.

The Declaration of Independence is a seminal document of the United States. It explains why the people in Britain’s North American colonies wanted to create their own country with their own laws.
