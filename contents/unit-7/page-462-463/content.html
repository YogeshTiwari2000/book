28 “Worman, I done a lot of thinking as that bus rolled along the highway. I would eat a chocolate and think over my experience with Beryl. Things kind of cleared up in my mind, like having blinders taken off of my eyes. I saw I had really been ignorant of some things that other folks knowed. I didn’t talk right to suit some of them, but that wasn’t my fault. I didn’t know we was all supposed to talk the same way. I thought people hadn’t all talked the same since before God tore down their tower at Babel and confused all their tongues. Folks all over the world have talked different ever since then. I guess some of them like Beryl want to go back to pre-Babel days. Anyway, it was sure an eye-opener to me, hurt me, too. Beryl just plain separated herself from me. It was like she took a sharp knife and cut a melon in half, and throwed away the half that was me. You know what you do with a piece of melon you don’t want. You throw it with the rinds into the garbage can. Worman, who said that we all have to talk alike? Can anyone tell me that?”

GLOSSARY

Exploring Pathways: Copywriter

Businesses are always in need of strong writers. A copywriter’s job is to write copy (text). Copywriters must think of their audience and adjust their tone to capture the audience. They must also think of what the business is trying to achieve in the communication. Copywriters often work with others to incorporate ideas and improve their voice.
