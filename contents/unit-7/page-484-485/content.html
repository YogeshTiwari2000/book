Marketing

▲ The goal of marketing is to interest customers in buying a product or service.

Marketing is the process of promoting (letting the world know about) a product or service. Its goal is to make people interested in using or buying the product or service. Nearly every kind of business or organization that sells a product or offers a service needs to do marketing. Marketing has five career pathways:

  • Marketing Communications
  • Marketing Management
  • Marketing Research
  • Merchandising
  • Professional Sales

6 Tips for Marketers:

Design Ads to Get Results

Marketers think about a product or service a company wants to sell. They also consider their audience, that is, the people who might be interested in buying that product or service. People who work in marketing use this information to create print and online advertisements, or ads. Ads help the audience to learn more about the product or service the company is offering. Our marketing team has put together six principles for designing effective advertisements.

1 Grab their attention.

With a large image and big text, you can get people’s attention so they remember your message. Use a striking photo or illustration. Carefully consider the font you are using as well as the size of the text.

2 Focus on the problem you are solving.

While brainstorming an ad concept or idea, think about the problem you are solving for your audience or customer. What is the solution you are proposing? Make your solution clear and easy to understand.

3 Keep it short and simple.

What is the most important thing you want to say to the customer? (See Tip 2). Articulate your main point into a simple, short, and clear message.
