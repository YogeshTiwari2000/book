7

The Power of Communication

Essential Question

How do you know when communication is effective?

Note It!

List different types of people you communicate with (for example: friends, siblings, teacher, boss, coach). Think about how you communicate differently with each.

Engage with the Theme

Watch some friends talk about the power of communication.

What You Bring

Talk about different ways that you communicate your thoughts and feelings. Think about communication through:

  • speaking
  • music
  • writing
  • videos
  • actions
  • social media
  • art

From Your Perspective

Talk about what you can do to communicate your thoughts and feeling more effectively. For example:

  • How can you communicate more clearly?
  • How can you communicate more often?
  • How can you communicate in different ways?
