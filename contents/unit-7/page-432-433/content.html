Expand Vocabulary Morphemic Analysis: Affixes and Roots

When you read, you will encounter words you don’t know. Morphemic analysis can help you determine what the word means. Separate words into morphemes to find clues about the meaning.

inevitable: in + evit + able

The prefix in- means not.

The root -evit- means to avoid.

The suffix -able indicates the word is an adjective.

Analyze suffixes to determine the part of speech. Knowing the part of speech can help you determine the meaning of the word.

suffix part of speech examples
-tion, -acy, -ism, -y, -ity, -ment, -ness noun opposition, democracy, patriotism, theory, government, sincerity, unfairness
-able, -al, -ful, -ic, -ous adjective inevitable, national, careful, democratic, obvious
-ly adverb unfailingly, stupidly
-ify, -ate, -ize, -en verb justify, communicate, synthesize, widen
