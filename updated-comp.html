Genre Infographic

Infographics are visuals that represent information graphically. Infographics:

  • present facts clearly
  • present information quickly
  • use patterns of data to reinforce understanding
  • try to engage readers’ interest
  • may use charts and graphs to present data

The purpose of an infographic is to inform.

reading-strategy

READING STRATEGY
Use Real-World Knowledge

As you listen, use your own knowledge of the real world to deepen your understanding of the topic. Think about what you know and what you have learned about the topic in the past. Connect the details of the talk to the details of your everyday life. When the details connect to your own life, the topic may become more understandable and important to you.

    1

    In 1824, Louis Braille began to work on a writing system that would allow the blind to read. He was just 15 years old.

    2

    At the time, he was studying at a school for the blind in Paris, France. In his classes, teachers were using books with an older ineffective writing system. The system he developed, called Braille, is now the standard.

    3

    Schools today are using Braille, and all over the world Louis Braille’s work is benefiting the blind. At the same time, people are developing new technologies to further improve life for visually impaired people. Many people believe that in the future we all will be communicating in new ways.
